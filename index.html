Fragment SAR Database

FragmentSAR is a cheminformatics platform for exploring structure–activity relationships (SAR) across chemical fragments, molecules, and protein targets.

It connects fragment scaffolds to their parent molecules and visualises bioactivity through interactive networks and searchable data tables.
